Measuring Matter Antimatter Asymmetries at the LHC

This project investigated CP violation by analysing B-meson decays using data from the LHCb experiment. The study identified significant matter-antimatter asymmetries, which suggest that hadron rescattering plays a role in generating these violations. The findings contribute to understanding the imbalance of matter and antimatter in the universe. The project also proposes methodological improvements for future analyses to enhance the precision of CP violation measurements.

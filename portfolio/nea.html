To what extent do COVID-19 mortality rates and their perception correlate with relative deprivation in Greater London?

This investigation analysed how COVID-19 mortality rates correlate with deprivation in Greater London. Using data from the Office for National Statistics and survey responses, the study revealed that deprived areas had higher mortality rates. It also uncovered differences in how affluent and deprived communities perceived these risks, highlighting the socio-economic complexities of health outcomes.
